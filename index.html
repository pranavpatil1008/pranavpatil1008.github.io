About Me

Who I Am

I’m a driven and adaptable graduate student pursuing an M.S. in Information Science at Indiana University's Luddy School, expecting to graduate in December 2025. My academic and professional journey is fueled by a deep-seated passion for leveraging technology and data to create meaningful, positive impact in the world. With a strong foundation built upon rigorous coursework and practical experience in data analysis, disinformation research, and human-centered computing, I continuously seek opportunities to expand my knowledge and refine my skills.

My background includes significant leadership experience, notably managing diverse teams of over 100 staff members at Indiana University, where I honed my skills in mentorship, operational efficiency, and fostering collaborative environments. Complementing this is my entrepreneurial spirit, demonstrated through founding and managing a real estate consultancy in India. I'm adept at navigating complex challenges, proven by successfully managing cross-border communications and operations during the global pandemic and delving into specialized intelligence studies focused on hybrid warfare and deception tactics.

My Philosophy

I firmly believe in a human-centric approach to technology. For me, effective solutions must transcend mere efficiency; they must be intuitive, ethical, accessible, and ultimately enhance the human experience. My goal is to act as a bridge between the rapidly advancing frontier of technical possibilities and the nuanced spectrum of human needs, ensuring that innovation serves a genuine purpose and promotes equitable outcomes.

In the dynamic landscape of technology, continuous learning, intellectual curiosity, and adaptability are not just beneficial—they are essential. I embrace challenges as opportunities for growth and actively seek out diverse perspectives to inform my approach to problem-solving.

Core Values

  • Integrity: Upholding ethical principles in all aspects of work and life.
  • Collaboration: Believing that the best solutions arise from diverse teams working together.
  • Impact: Striving to create technology and solutions that make a tangible, positive difference.
  • Curiosity: Maintaining a constant desire to learn, explore, and understand.
  • Empathy: Designing and leading with a deep understanding of user needs and perspectives.

Interests & Hobbies

Beyond the realms of academics and professional work, I am deeply fascinated by the transformative potential of Artificial Intelligence and its complex societal implications. I actively follow developments in machine learning, natural language processing, and AI ethics. Exploring emerging technologies, from blockchain concepts to advances in virtual reality, keeps my perspective fresh.

In my downtime, I enjoy engaging in strategic board games that challenge critical thinking, contributing to local community initiatives, and unwinding with landscape photography. I'm also an avid reader, particularly drawn to topics related to behavioral economics, global affairs, and science fiction. Being fluent in English, Hindi, and Marathi allows me to connect with and appreciate diverse cultures and perspectives more deeply.
